Privacy Policy

Road Beans — Last updated April 2026

Road Beans is a personal coffee journal app. Your privacy matters. Here is exactly what we do and do not do with your data.

Data we collect

Road Beans stores the data you enter: coffee shop visits, drink ratings, photos, tags, and notes. This data lives on your device and, if you are signed in to iCloud, in your private iCloud account.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect analytics, crash reports, advertising identifiers, or any personal information. We have no servers. We do not receive any data you enter into Road Beans.

iCloud sync

If you enable iCloud, your data is synced through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure using your personal iCloud account. Apple's privacy policy governs that storage. We cannot access it.

Location

Road Beans requests your location only to help you find nearby coffee shops. Location data is used on-device and is not transmitted anywhere.

Photos

Photos you add to visits are stored on your device and in your iCloud account if sync is enabled. They are not shared or uploaded to any third-party service.

Third parties

Road Beans uses Apple's MapKit for maps and location search. No other third-party SDKs or services are included.

Children

Road Beans does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
