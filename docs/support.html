Frequently asked questions

How do I back up my data?

Go to the Backup tab and tap Prepare Export. This creates a JSON file with all your visits, places, drinks, and tags that you can save or share anywhere.

Is my data backed up to iCloud?

Yes, if you are signed in to iCloud on your device, Road Beans syncs your data automatically. You do not need to do anything to enable this.

I reinstalled the app and my data is gone

If you were signed in to iCloud before reinstalling, your data will sync back automatically after you open the app and sign in to the same iCloud account. Allow a few minutes for the sync to complete.

How do I delete a visit?

Open the visit detail and scroll to the bottom. Tap Delete Visit.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Go to the Backup tab and tap Prepare Export to generate a full JSON backup of your data.
